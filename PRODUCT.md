# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters and technical hiring managers scanning for senior backend engineers at remote-first companies. Freelance clients and startup founders looking to contract a senior engineer who can own a project end to end. Occasional inbound from conference/community discovery.

Context: fast scan, high signal-to-noise tolerance is low. They open 10-20 profiles per session. The job is to stand out through precision, not personality.

## Product Purpose

A conversion-focused portfolio for Sanket Saboo, Senior Software Engineer at BrowserStack. Primary goal: land remote full-time roles, freelance contracts, and consulting engagements. Secondary goal: establish credibility as someone who operates at scale (1.2M+ production systems, enterprise clients).

Success = an inbound message or application decision made before the visitor reaches the footer.

## Brand Personality

Refined. Crafted. Precise.

Voice: direct and specific. No abstractions. Numbers over adjectives. The engineer speaks, not the marketer.

Emotional goal: the visitor should feel they are looking at work from someone who is already operating at a higher level than the role they are hiring for.

## Anti-references

- Generic dev portfolio templates (Three.js globe, particle backgrounds, GitHub-stats card layout, the "dark blue + yellow accent" SWE template).
- Agency / Awwwards-style work: scroll hijacking, cursor effects, excessive animation, style over substance.
- SaaS marketing sites: purple gradients, feature-icon grids, testimonial carousels with star ratings.
- Cluttered or information-dense: too many sections, wall-of-text descriptions, sidebar skill bars.

## Design Principles

1. **Let the work prove it.** Every claim is backed by a specific number or outcome. No "passionate about building great products." Show 1.2M websites. Show <0.0001% error rate.
2. **Quiet authority.** Premium through restraint, not decoration. Confidence does not need to announce itself. The design should feel like it already has the job.
3. **Every pixel earned.** Nothing on the page that does not serve conversion. No filler sections, no placeholder copy, no decorative patterns that carry no meaning.
4. **Senior from first glance.** Typography scale, spatial rhythm, and information hierarchy should signal seniority before the visitor reads a single word.
5. **Precision over personality.** Backend engineers are valued for reliability and exactness. The design should feel the same way: exact, measured, nothing out of place.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Reduced-motion respected for all animations. Color is never the sole carrier of meaning. Keyboard navigation on all interactive elements.
